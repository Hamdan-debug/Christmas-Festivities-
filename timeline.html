Highlights from Christmas Past

🎅 2024

The first-ever AI-driven Christmas caroling event went viral, with robots singing carols in harmony.

🎄 2020

During the global pandemic, families turned to virtual Christmas celebrations, making video calls a staple of the season.

🌟 1914

World War I Christmas Truce: Soldiers from opposing sides in the trenches came together to sing carols and exchange small gifts.

🎁 1843

Charles Dickens published A Christmas Carol, shaping many modern Christmas traditions and themes.

🎄 336 AD

The first recorded Christmas celebration took place in Rome, marking the birth of Jesus Christ on December 25.
